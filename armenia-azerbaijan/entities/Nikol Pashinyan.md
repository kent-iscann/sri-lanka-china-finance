# Nikol Pashinyan

**Role:** Prime Minister of Armenia (since 2018)
**Party:** Civil Contract

## Profile
Pashinyan came to power in 2018 through the Velvet Revolution, a peaceful protest movement that ousted the long-ruling Republican Party. A former journalist and opposition figure, he positioned himself as a reformer committed to anti-corruption, democratic governance, and a foreign policy pivot toward the West.

## Key Positions
- **Peace with Azerbaijan:** Has consistently signaled willingness to recognize Azerbaijani sovereignty over Nagorno-Karabakh in exchange for security guarantees and a comprehensive peace treaty. This stance is deeply unpopular among many Armenians who view it as capitulation.
- **Western Pivot:** Has systematically distanced Armenia from Russia, hosting EU and Ukrainian leaders, signing strategic partnerships with the US, and pursuing normalization with Turkey.
- **Domocratic Reform:** Has pledged to reform Armenia's institutions, including controversial plans to reform the Armenian Apostolic Church's governance.

## Political Situation
Following the June 2026 parliamentary elections, Pashinyan's Civil Contract party won 49.8% of votes — enough for a parliamentary majority. The election was marked by documented Russian interference aimed at bolstering pro-Russian opposition parties. Pashinyan overcame a pressure campaign by Moscow and won a mandate to continue peace talks with Azerbaijan and the Western pivot.

## Key Relationships
- **Ilham Aliyev (Azerbaijan):** Negotiating partner for peace treaty. Relations remain tense but both sides have committed to a diplomatic process.
- **Vladimir Putin (Russia):** Relations have deteriorated sharply. Putin has publicly warned Pashinyan against the Western pivot, comparing it to Ukraine's path before 2014.
- **Recep Tayyip Erdoğan (Turkey):** Normalization process ongoing. Trade resumed in May 2026 after 33 years. Border remains closed but railway links have reopened.
- **Donald Trump (US):** Trump brokered the August 2025 peace framework. The TRUMP Route for International Peace and Prosperity (TRIPP) corridor is a key US-backed initiative.
