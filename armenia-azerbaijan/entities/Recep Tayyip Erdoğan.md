# Recep Tayyip Erdoğan

**Role:** President of Turkey (since 2014)

## Relevance to Armenia-Azerbaijan
Turkey is Azerbaijan's closest strategic ally and was instrumental in Azerbaijan's 2020 military victory in Nagorno-Karabakh. Turkey shut its border with Armenia in 1993 in solidarity with Azerbaijan during the first war.

## Normalization with Armenia
In 2026, Turkey took concrete steps toward normalizing relations with Armenia:
- **May 11, 2026:** Turkey lifted restrictions on direct trade declarations with Armenia after 33 years
- **June 3, 2026:** Historic Armenia-Turkey business forum held in Kars
- Railway connections through Georgia reopened for Armenian imports/exports

Turkey's normalization is driven by the new power reality after Azerbaijan's victory — Ankara can afford to be flexible toward Yerevan without jeopardizing its partnership with Baku.

## Key Constraint
The border remains closed. Full diplomatic relations have not been restored. Turkey's normalization is conditional on Armenia's peace process with Azerbaijan and is closely coordinated with Baku.
